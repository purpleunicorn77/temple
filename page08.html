Buddha said: "Volition I call Kamma. Through volition, one performs action by body, by speech, or by mind."

Kamma is the cause, not the effect.

Wise attention will allow you to do good Kamma.

Forests nourishing creatures, respecting lives is very important.

Unwise attention will cause you to do bad kamma.

The future results or fruit will depend on your volition, in the present.

Disrepecting lives would get bad results.
